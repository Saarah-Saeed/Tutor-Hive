OUR STORY

From a Simple Idea to a Learning Revolution

TutorHive was born in 2022 from a dorm room frustration — finding a good tutor was slow, expensive, and hit-or-miss. Our founders believed there had to be a better way.

Today, TutorHive connects thousands of students with vetted tutors across 50+ subjects, offering flexible scheduling, live sessions, and real results.
